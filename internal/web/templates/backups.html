Restoring

A backup is encrypted with your master key, so it's safe to download and store off-site — restoring needs that same key. To recover onto a fresh server: install Helmsman with the same encryption_key, then restore the snapshot with the CLI:

helmsman restore --from helmsman-backup-<id>.hmbk

Restoring replaces Helmsman's database, so it's a deliberate CLI step rather than a dashboard button. Your apps' own data volumes are backed up separately (see the docs).
