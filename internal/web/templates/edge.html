Edge routes

Helmsman owns the public edge (HTTPS + automatic certificates) and renders the whole edge config from your apps' helmsman.yaml. Routes are declared per app under spec.edge.routes — a hostname → an app service:port — and applied on deploy. The edge can never reach the control plane (control-plane ports are refused).

Read-only — edit routes in your app's helmsman.yaml (spec.edge.routes) and deploy. This page shows the routes from the last deploy.
